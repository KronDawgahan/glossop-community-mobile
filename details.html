How to sign up

We've kept it as simple as possible. No lengthy processes, no confusing terms.

  1. 1
    Choose your plan

    Pick the data allowance that suits you. All plans include unlimited UK calls and texts.

  2. 2
    Enter your details

    Contact and billing details — quick and straightforward.

  3. 3
    Keep your number (if you want)

    Text 150 and request a PAC code from your current provider. Enter it at sign-up. Your number transfers with no downtime.

  4. 4
    Get your SIM — delivered on Friday

    We'll deliver your new SIM. Or if your phone supports eSIM, you can be up and running in minutes. Activate by entering your SIM code at our site.

  5. 5
    You're away — and making an impact

    From your very first bill, part of your spend goes to the Glossop Community Pot.

Community Payback

Glossop Community Mobile is structured so that all operating profits are directed back into the local community. This isn't a donation scheme bolted onto a commercial business — it's built into the legal structure of our Community Interest Company.

And as membership grows, we can give back more per member. Here's our published commitment:

About Community Mobile CIC

Glossop Community Mobile is operated by Community Mobile CIC — a Community Interest Company formed specifically to provide community-branded SIM and eSIM mobile services to places like ours.

Our mission

To deliver maximum social, community and environmental benefit — by providing a quality mobile service and returning all profit to the communities it serves. We believe communities know best where to allocate funds generated by their own mobile service.

Our purpose

Market forces and technology are increasingly distancing services from communities and channelling wealth upwards. Community Mobile CIC uses technology to realise high-quality services at a community level, and redirects the wealth generated back into those communities.

Why Glossop?

Quite simply, because we are Glossopians. Glossop Community Mobile is our first brand. Glossop is a proud, tight-knit community and the ideal place to show what community mobile can do. If it works here, we hope to grow the model to other communities — which itself increases how much we can give back.

Our promise

Always be transparent. Monthly payment reconciliations and reports are published. Asset-lock agreements are in place. Impact is measurable, auditable, and shared openly with every member.

Privacy Notice

What data we collect: When you pre-register, we collect your email address and, optionally, the month your current mobile contract expires.

Why we collect it: Solely to contact you when Glossop Community Mobile launches. We will send a maximum of two emails: one to notify you the service is live, and one with full details on how to join.

What we won't do: We will not share your data with any third party, use it for marketing, sell it, or retain it beyond the launch period.

Your rights: You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing glossopcommunitymobile@gmail.com.

Data controller: Community Mobile CIC. Full ICO registration details to follow at launch.

This is a pre-launch privacy notice. A full UK GDPR-compliant privacy policy will be published when the service goes live.
