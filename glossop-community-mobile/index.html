The more members, the more we give back

We're transparent about how community payback works. As Glossop Community Mobile grows, the amount returned per member increases. Everyone benefits from spreading the word.

At just 200 members that's already £400/month — around £5,000 a year — going back into Glossop.

Where the money goes

Our members help decide. Here are the kinds of causes we're here to fund:

  • 🌿 Nature restoration & Peak District conservation
  • 🤲 Social programmes — befriending, life skills, social prescribing
  • 🛝 Local play areas, clubs & activities for young people
  • ⚡ Community energy
  • 🌸 Glossop beautification
  • 🏃 Community groups, clubs & teams
  • 🎉 Local events to bring people together
  • 🥫 Food banks & donation centres
  • 📢 Funding local campaigns
  • 💡 Something else — you tell us

All funds are asset-locked to agreed projects. We publish monthly impact reports so you can see exactly where your money goes.
